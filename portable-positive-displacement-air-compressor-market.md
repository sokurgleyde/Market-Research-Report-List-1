Oil-Free compressors utilize advanced technology to minimize lubrication, making them ideal for applications requiring clean air, such as in food processing and medical fields. In contrast, Oil Immersed compressors use oil for lubrication, enhancing durability and allowing for higher pressure outputs, making them suitable for heavy-duty tasks in industrial and construction settings. Each type caters to specific needs and operational environments.</p></p>
