<p><p>The smart toilet market is categorized into two main types: smart toilet seats and integrated smart toilets. Smart toilet seats are retrofitted onto existing toilets, offering features such as heated seating, water bidets, and customizable cleansing functions. In contrast, integrated smart toilets combine these functionalities into a single unit, featuring built-in bidets, drying capabilities, and touchless controls. Both types enhance comfort, hygiene, and convenience, catering to modern consumer demands for innovative bathroom solutions.</p></p>
