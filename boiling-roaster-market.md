<p><strong>The Boiling Roaster Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gas Enters From The Bottom</li><li>Gas Enters From The Top</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Boiling Roaster market is categorized into two main types based on gas entry points: gas enters from the bottom and gas enters from the top. In the bottom-entry design, gas heats the roasting chamber from beneath, allowing for efficient and even cooking, ideal for large batches. Conversely, the top-entry model introduces gas from above, promoting convection and enhancing flavor development. Each type offers unique advantages suited to various roasting requirements and operational preferences, catering to diverse culinary needs.</p></p>
