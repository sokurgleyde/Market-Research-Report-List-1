<p><p>The automotive HVAC market encompasses two primary types: Manual HVAC and Automatic HVAC systems. Manual HVAC allows drivers and passengers to manually adjust temperature and airflow settings, providing basic climate control. In contrast, Automatic HVAC systems utilize advanced sensors and controls to automatically regulate interior climate, maintaining desired temperatures with minimal user input. Automatic systems enhance convenience and comfort, often including features like dual-zone temperature control and air quality management, making them increasingly popular in modern vehicles.</p></p>
