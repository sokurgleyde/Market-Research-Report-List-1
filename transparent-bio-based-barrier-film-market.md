<p><p>The Transparent Bio-Based Barrier Film market comprises two main types: monolayer and multilayer films. Monolayer films are made from a single layer of biodegradable materials, offering straightforward production and recycling options, while multilayer films integrate multiple layers of different bio-based materials, enhancing barrier properties and durability. Multilayer films are particularly effective for applications requiring superior moisture, gas, and UV protection, making them suitable for food packaging and other sensitive products. Both types aim to provide sustainable and eco-friendly packaging solutions.</p></p>
