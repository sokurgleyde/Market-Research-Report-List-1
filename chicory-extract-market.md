<p><p>The chicory extract market is primarily categorized into two types: powder extract and liquid extract. Powder extracts are produced by dehydrating chicory roots and grinding them into a fine powder, suitable for dietary supplements, food additives, and functional foods. Liquid extracts, on the other hand, involve extracting the active compounds from chicory through solvents, offering concentrated formulations for beverages, herbal remedies, and nutritional products. Both types are valued for their prebiotic properties and health benefits, catering to diverse consumer needs.</p></p>
