<p><p>The anti-reflection coatings market is categorized into two main types: single-layer AR coatings and multi-layer AR coatings. Single-layer AR coatings consist of a single film applied to a substrate, typically offering limited reduction in reflection but being cost-effective. In contrast, multi-layer AR coatings feature several thin layers designed to minimize reflections across a broader range of wavelengths, enhancing efficiency and performance significantly. This duality caters to diverse applications, from consumer electronics to optical instruments, addressing varying optical requirements.</p></p>
