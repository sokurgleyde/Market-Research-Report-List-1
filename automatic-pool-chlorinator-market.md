<p><strong>The Automatic Pool Chlorinator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inline Pool Chlorinator</li><li>Offline Pool Chlorinator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic pool chlorinator market is divided into two main types: inline and offline pool chlorinators. Inline pool chlorinators are integrated directly into the plumbing system, allowing for continuous and automatic chlorination as water flows through the filter. In contrast, offline pool chlorinators operate independently, using a separate container to hold and dissolve chlorine tablets before releasing chlorine into the pool water. Both types enhance water quality by maintaining appropriate chlorine levels, simplifying pool maintenance for users.</p></p>
