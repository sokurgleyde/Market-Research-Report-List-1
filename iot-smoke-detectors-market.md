<p><p>The IoT smoke detectors market features three primary types: photoelectric, ionization, and combination detectors. Photoelectric detectors utilize a light beam to sense smoke, making them effective for smoldering fires. Ionization detectors employ radioactive material to detect fast-flaming fires, responding quickly to small particles. Combination detectors merge both technologies, offering enhanced protection by addressing various fire scenarios. With IoT capabilities, these detectors can provide real-time alerts and remote monitoring, improving safety and enabling prompt responses in emergencies.</p></p>
