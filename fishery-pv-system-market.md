In the floating type market, PV panels are installed on floating platforms over water bodies, allowing for simultaneous fish farming and energy production while optimizing space. The fixed type market involves solar panels mounted on land adjacent to fish farms, requiring more land but offering easier maintenance and monitoring. Both systems aim to enhance sustainability by efficiently utilizing resources, reducing land competition, and promoting renewable energy alongside aquaculture practices.</p></p>
