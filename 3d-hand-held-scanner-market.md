Laser scanners utilize laser beams to capture precise measurements of an objectâ€™s surface, providing high accuracy and detail, making them ideal for intricate designs. Conversely, structured light scanners project a series of light patterns onto the object and analyze the deformations of these patterns to create 3D models. While laser scanners excel in capturing depth and texture, structured light scanners are effective in speed and capturing complex shapes.</p></p>
