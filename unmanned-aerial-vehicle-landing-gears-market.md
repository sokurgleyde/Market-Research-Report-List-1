<p><p>The Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV) landing gears market features various types designed for specific operational needs. Pontoon landing gear is tailored for aquatic operations, enabling water landings and takeoffs. Framed landing gear offers lightweight flexibility and is often used in racing drones. Each type emphasizes different performance characteristics suited for diverse UAV applications.</p></p>
