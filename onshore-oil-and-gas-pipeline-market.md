<p><p>The onshore oil and gas pipeline market comprises several types of pipes used for transporting hydrocarbons. ERW (Electric Resistance Welded) pipes are preferred for low-pressure applications due to their strength and cost-effectiveness. SSAW (Spiral Submerged Arc Welded) pipes are designed for larger diameters and offer high resistance to pressure. LSAW (Longitudinal Submerged Arc Welded) pipes are ideal for high-pressure environments, ensuring structural integrity. Other pipes include seamless and coated options, catering to various operational requirements in diverse terrains.</p></p>
