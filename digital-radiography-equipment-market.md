<p><ul><li>CR Tech Digital X-Ray System</li><li>DR Tech Digital X-Ray System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Digital Radiography Equipment market encompasses two main types: Computed Radiography (CR) and Digital Radiography (DR). CR Tech Digital X-Ray Systems utilize imaging plates to capture data, which is then processed on a computer, offering a cost-effective solution for facilities transitioning from traditional film. In contrast, DR Tech Digital X-Ray Systems employ direct digital detectors to produce immediate images with higher quality and efficiency, making them ideal for high-volume environments. Both technologies play vital roles in modern medical imaging.</p></p>
