<p><strong>The Reinforced Carbon-carbon (RCC) Composite Materials Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Liquid Impregnation Process</li><li>Chemical Vapor Deposition</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Reinforced Carbon-Carbon (RCC) composite materials are primarily categorized based on their production methods. The Liquid Impregnation Process involves infiltrating carbon fibers with a liquid resin to enhance structural integrity and thermal resistance. In contrast, the Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD) method applies a gaseous precursor that bonds carbon materials on the fiber surface, forming a dense, high-performance layer. Both techniques are crucial in producing RCC materials, commonly used in aerospace and high-temperature applications due to their superior strength and thermal stability.</p></p>
