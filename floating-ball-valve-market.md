<p><p>A Floating Ball Valve is a type of valve that features a sphere, or ball, with a hole through its center. This ball is allowed to float within the valve body, providing a tight seal against the valve’s seats when pressure is applied from the upstream side. Its design allows for quick operation and leak-tight sealing, making it ideal for various applications across industries such as oil and gas, water and wastewater, chemicals, and power generation.</p><p>The Floating Ball Valve Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.8% during the forecast period, driven by increasing demand for efficient flow control solutions in diverse industrial applications. Emerging economies are investing in infrastructural development, contributing to the rising adoption of floating ball valves in new projects. Additionally, advancements in valve technology, such as corrosion-resistant materials and automation capabilities, are shaping market trends. Furthermore, environmental regulations are pushing for the use of high-performance valves to minimize leakages and emissions. The growth of renewable energy sources, particularly in power generation, is also expected to further boost the market, leading to a wider acceptance of floating ball valves in modern systems.</p></p>
