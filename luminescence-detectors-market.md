<p><p>The luminescence detectors market is primarily segmented into tube luminescence detectors and microplate luminescence detectors. Tube luminescence detectors are designed for analyzing samples in test tubes, offering high sensitivity and specificity for various applications, including environmental and biological testing. In contrast, microplate luminescence detectors facilitate high-throughput screening by analyzing samples in multi-well plates, making them suitable for screening assays in drug discovery and clinical research. Both types enhance detection capabilities across various scientific fields, optimizing research and diagnostics.</p></p>
