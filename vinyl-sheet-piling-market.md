<p><p>The vinyl sheet piling market primarily consists of two types: VZ type and VU type. VZ type features a varying thickness design that enhances resistance against soil pressure, making it suitable for deep-water applications and heavy-duty use. In contrast, VU type has a uniform thickness, providing versatility for lighter applications such as residential and recreational projects. Both types are durable, corrosion-resistant, and environmentally friendly, offering long-lasting solutions for construction, waterfront stabilization, and landscape enhancement.</p></p>
