<p><p>High side and low side switches are essential components in electronic circuits, enabling control over power delivery to loads such as motors, lights, and other devices. High side switches are positioned between the power supply and the load, allowing the load to be connected to the positive supply voltage. Conversely, low side switches are situated between the load and ground, controlling power through the ground connection. </p><p>The High Side and Low Side Switches Market is poised for significant growth, with an expected compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 5.1% during the forecast period.
