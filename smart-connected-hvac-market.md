<p><strong>The Smart Connected HVAC Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Split Systems</li><li>Multi Split Systems</li><li>VRF Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Smart Connected HVAC market comprises three main types: Single Split Systems, Multi Split Systems, and VRF Systems. Single Split Systems consist of an individual indoor and outdoor unit, ideal for small spaces. Multi Split Systems connect multiple indoor units to one outdoor unit, allowing flexibility and energy efficiency for medium-sized buildings. VRF (Variable Refrigerant Flow) Systems provide advanced control over multiple zones, optimizing energy use and comfort for large commercial spaces, making them highly sought after in modern HVAC solutions.</p></p>
