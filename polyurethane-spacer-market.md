<p><p>The polyurethane spacer market is divided into two main types: soft and hard spacers. Soft spacers are flexible and compressible, making them ideal for applications requiring cushioning and insulation, such as in automotive and construction industries. In contrast, hard spacers are rigid and provide structural support, often used in applications like mechanical components and electronics. Each type serves distinct purposes, catering to varying industry needs based on cushioning, insulation, durability, and load-bearing requirements.</p></p>
