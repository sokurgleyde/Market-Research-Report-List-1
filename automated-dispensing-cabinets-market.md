<p><p>Automated Dispensing Cabinets (ADCs) are classified into two main types: centralized and decentralized. Centralized ADCs are typically located in a pharmacy or central medication area, allowing for bulk storage and inventory management, while streamlining medication distribution to various departments. In contrast, decentralized ADCs are positioned closer to patient care areas, such as nursing stations, enhancing immediate access to medications for healthcare providers. Both types aim to optimize medication safety, improve efficiency, and reduce human error in the medication dispensing process.</p></p>
