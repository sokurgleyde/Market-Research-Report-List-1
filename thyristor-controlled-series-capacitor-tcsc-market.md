<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Thyristor Controlled Series Capacitor (TCSC) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Blocking Mode</li><li>Bypass Mode</li><li>Capacitive Boost Mode (Capacitive Vernier Mode)</li><li>Inductive Boost Mode (Inductive Vernier Mode)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Thyristor Controlled Series Capacitor (TCSC) market involves several operation modes influencing power flow in transmission systems. In Blocking Mode, the thyristor is turned off, preventing capacitor operation. Bypass Mode allows bypassing the capacitor for system stability. Capacitive Boost Mode enhances capacity for dynamic stability, while Inductive Boost Mode introduces inductance for reactive power correction. Each mode serves distinct operational requirements, optimizing transmission efficiency and reliability in various power system scenarios, fostering enhanced grid performance and integration of renewable energy sources.</p></p>
