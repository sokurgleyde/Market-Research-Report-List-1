<p><p>The Liquid-applied Elastic Waterproofing Membrane market comprises two primary types: Cold-Applied and Hot-Applied Membranes. Cold-Applied Liquid Waterproofing Membranes use solvents or water-based formulations that cure at ambient temperatures, providing ease of application and quick drying times. In contrast, Hot-Applied Liquid Waterproofing Membranes are heated before application, offering superior adhesion and durability. Both types are used to create seamless, flexible barriers against water infiltration, catering to diverse construction and infrastructure projects based on specific environmental conditions and performance requirements.</p></p>
