<p><p>The Silver Coated Copper Powders market comprises two primary types: Spherical Silver Coated Copper Powders and Flake Silver Coated Copper Powders. Spherical powders are characterized by their uniform shape, enhancing flowability and packing density, making them suitable for applications in electronics and conductive inks. Flake powders, on the other hand, feature a flat, thin structure that provides a larger surface area, improving conductivity and adhesion properties, often used in coatings and conductive pastes. Both types cater to diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
