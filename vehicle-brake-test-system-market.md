<p><p>The vehicle brake test system market includes various types designed to evaluate braking performance. Roller brake test machines use rollers to simulate road conditions, measuring brake force and balance. Plate brake test machines employ a flat plate to assess braking effectiveness, often used for heavy vehicles. Decelerometers, on the other hand, are portable devices that measure deceleration during a vehicle's braking process, providing insights into braking efficiency and safety. Each type serves essential roles in ensuring vehicle safety and compliance with regulations.</p></p>
