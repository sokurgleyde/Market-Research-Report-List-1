<p><ul><li>Clipped Head Framing Nailer</li><li>Round Head Framing Nailer</li><li>Coil Framing Nailer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The framing nailer market comprises several types catering to different construction needs. Clipped head framing nailers use a staggered nail design that enables quicker action and easier access in tight spaces, making them ideal for framing applications. Round head framing nailers, featuring a round nail head, provide superior holding strength and are often preferred for structural integrity. Coil framing nailers utilize coiled nails for continuous firing, offering high productivity and reduced reloading time, making them suitable for extensive projects.</p></p>
