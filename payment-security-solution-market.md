Tokenization replaces sensitive data with unique identifiers or tokens, reducing the risk of data breaches. Fraud Detection and Prevention systems utilize algorithms and machine learning to identify and mitigate potential fraudulent activities in real-time, safeguarding both businesses and consumers from financial losses and enhancing overall trust in payment systems.</p></p>
