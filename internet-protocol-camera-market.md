<p><ul><li>Centralized Internet Protocol Camera</li><li>Decentralized Internet Protocol Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Internet Protocol Camera market is divided into centralized and decentralized types. Centralized IP cameras rely on a single server to manage video feeds, offering easier integration and management, making them suitable for large installations. Conversely, decentralized IP cameras operate independently, processing and storing data on-site, which enhances security and reduces bandwidth dependence. This decentralized approach allows for greater flexibility in smaller or distributed setups where direct control and immediate access to footage are essential.</p></p>
