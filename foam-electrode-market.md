<p><p>The foam electrode market encompasses two primary types: Ag sensors and AgCl sensors. Ag sensors utilize silver for conductivity, often providing stable readings in various applications, especially in biomedical fields. Conversely, AgCl sensors incorporate silver chloride, offering enhanced stability and reduced noise levels, making them ideal for long-term monitoring scenarios. Both types are crucial in electrochemical applications, including pH sensing and biochemical analysis, owing to their effectiveness in delivering accurate measurement and adaptability to diverse environments.</p></p>
