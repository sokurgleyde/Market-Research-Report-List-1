<p><p>The Positive Temperature Coefficient (PTC) thermistors market is primarily divided into two types: ceramic PTC and polymer PTC. Ceramic PTC thermistors are made from barium titanate and are known for their high stability and robust performance in applications like overcurrent protection and self-regulating heating. In contrast, polymer PTC thermistors utilize conductive polymers, offering flexibility and lower cost. They are typically used in applications such as automotive and consumer electronics, where lightweight and compact designs are essential. Both types serve critical roles in temperature regulation and safety.</p></p>
