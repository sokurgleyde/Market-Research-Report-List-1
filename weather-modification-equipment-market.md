<p><ul><li>Aerial Cloud Seeding Equipment</li><li>Ground-Based Cloud Seeding Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Weather Modification Equipment market encompasses various technologies designed to enhance precipitation through cloud seeding. Aerial cloud seeding equipment involves aircraft equipped with flares or spray systems that disperse seeding agents like silver iodide directly into clouds, effectively promoting rainfall. In contrast, ground-based cloud seeding equipment utilizes ground-based generators to release seeding materials into the atmosphere, often via pyrotechnics or aerosolized particles, targeting specific clouds from below. Both methods aim to increase rainfall and mitigate drought conditions.</p></p>
