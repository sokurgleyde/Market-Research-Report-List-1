<p><p>The ophthalmic diagnostic equipment market includes various specialized tools for eye examination and diagnosis. Fundus cameras capture detailed images of the retina, while ophthalmoscopes enable a direct view of the eye's interior. Refractors measure vision for corrective lenses, and Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT) provides cross-sectional imaging of retinal structures. Corneal topography systems map the surface of the cornea, slit lamps facilitate detailed observation of eye components, tonometers measure intraocular pressure, perimeters assess visual fields, and retinal ultrasound imaging offers additional insights into eye health.</p></p>
