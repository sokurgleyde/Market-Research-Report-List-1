<p><p>The Fabric Protection Agent market is categorized into Water-Based and Solvent-Based types. Water-Based agents utilize water as a primary solvent, offering lower toxicity and environmental impact, making them preferable for indoor use. They provide effective stain and dirt resistance while being easy to apply. In contrast, Solvent-Based agents rely on organic solvents, often delivering stronger protective properties against tough stains and chemicals. However, they may pose higher health risks and environmental concerns, impacting their market appeal and usage.</p></p>
