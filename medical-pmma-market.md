Sheets of PMMA serve in applications such as surgical drapes and protective barriers, offering clarity and ease of use. Microspheres are employed in drug delivery systems, enhancing the therapeutic effects of various medications through controlled release mechanisms. Together, these forms contribute to diverse medical applications within the healthcare sector.</p></p>
